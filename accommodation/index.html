    Accommodation

    Accommodation

    Singapore is one of Asia's largest trading hubs and a popular tourism destination. Accommodation is available for a wide range of budgets and tastes, and properties throughout the city are known for high service standards. Singapore's extensive MRT and bus networks also make it easy and affordable to stay beyond the immediate conference area.

    The hotels below were selected by the ICPP 2026 organizing committee for their proximity to the conference venue, NTU@one-north (11 Slim Barracks Rise, Singapore 138664). The nearest station is Buona Vista MRT (EW21). ICPP 2026 will be held in Singapore from September 28 - October 1, 2026.

    The easiest way to plan journeys by bus and train is with the official MyTransport.SG app. See MyTransport.SG for iOS, MyTransport.SG for Android, or the LTA MRT rail network map. For airport and venue directions, see Travel (How to Get There).

    Rates and availability subject to change

    Rates, room types, booking codes, and deadlines listed below are accurate at the time of publication but are not guaranteed. Please confirm current pricing and availability directly with each property at the time of booking.

    Special Rates for ICPP 2026 Participants

    The conference has arranged preferential rates with the following properties for ICPP 2026 attendees.

    About the \"++\" pricing

    Rates shown with \"++\" are per room, per night, and are subject to a 10% service charge and 9% GST on top of the quoted amount. You can use Wise's currency converter to estimate the cost in your local currency.

    Park Avenue Rochester

    Park Avenue Rochester is a short walk from Buona Vista MRT station and close to one-north, Singapore Science Park, International Business Park, and Mapletree Business City. The hotel has a swimming pool and gym, with numerous dining options nearby.

    31 Rochester Drive, Singapore 138637. Tel: +65 6809 7381.

    Inclusions: complimentary local calls and Wi-Fi. In addition, a two-way complimentary shuttle transfer between the hotel and NTU@one-north will be offered during the event dates if at least 20 rooms are materialised.

    Shuttle conditions

    Transfers must be arranged at least 72 hours in advance. Changes or cancellations must be communicated to the hotel at least 24 hours in advance. Passengers must be ready at the scheduled pickup time; waiting beyond the 10-minute grace period may incur a charge equivalent to a one-way trip, subject to GST. Special requirements such as child seats or wheelchair access must be requested in advance and are subject to availability.

    Suggested booking window: check-in 27 September 2026, check-out 2 October 2026.

    Cancellation policy

    Cancellation, shortening of stay, or no-show is subject to a charge for the full duration of the confirmed stay.
